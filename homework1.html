Tuwaiq 1000

The largest national initiative for the first time in the Kingdom to enable 1,000 young men and women through a camp to be held synchronously from the north to the south and from the east to the west over a period of 4-5 months with the aim of building and rehabilitating them according to international rules and requirements of the Saudi labor market.

About the camp

13 centers around the kingdom ,camp live broadcast ,Accredited certificates ,3-month camps

Registration steps

  1. Pass the admission test
  2. Final nomination for those accepted
  3. start camp

Camp Features

  • Qualification for the labor market
  • International trainers and experts
  • Accredited certificates
